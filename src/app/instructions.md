## CONTEXT (PRODUCT SUMMARY)
EasyFlow is an all-in-one operating system for ecommerce owners selling physical products with complex supply chains.

Target reality:
- products can have up to 50 components
- 10–15 suppliers per brand
- suppliers have different lead times, MOQ, and communication channels (email, portals, ecommerce)
- day-to-day work is chaotic: micro tasks appear constantly and get lost
- project management exists but is disconnected from real-time calendar execution
- restock planning is manual and error-prone, often causing out-of-stock or overstock

EasyFlow solves this with 2 pillars:
1) AI Calendar: capture tasks via chat/voice, auto-schedule, unify daily execution
2) Operations OS: Gantt integrated with calendar + Shopify integration + BOM + micro-Gantt per component + AI restock recommendations; confirm restock auto-generates supply chain tasks into the main Gantt and calendar.

Key principle:
**Everything converges into one timeline view.**
Calendar is the execution layer; Gantt is the strategy layer; BOM/micro-Gantt is the supply chain layer.
